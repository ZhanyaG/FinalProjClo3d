Wear less. Style more.

Switch looks instantly — AR/VR outfits on top of simple everyday clothes. Cleaner wardrobe, lower waste.

Quick facts

  • 944 million metric tons CO₂e — apparel industry emissions (recent industry estimate).
  • About 92 million tonnes of textile waste generated globally each year.
  • Only around 8–11% of textile fibres get recycled — most pieces end up in landfill or low-value uses.
  • The virtual try-on / avatar fashion market is already in the low-billions (USD) and growing rapidly — immersive commerce adoption is accelerating.

Why this matters

Digital outfits let people own many looks without buying, storing or washing extra garments. As AR/VR hardware and virtual wardrobe platforms mature, many will prefer to switch looks digitally and wear simpler, functional clothing in real life — reducing laundry, wear-and-tear and waste.
